Contacting the Dartmouth Climbing Gym

Let us know of any questions and/or feedback about any upcoming or previous visits. We'd love to hear from you!

Below you'll find ways to contact us at management, as well as a feedback form for us to get a better idea of how people are enjoying the gym.

*If you are inquiring about the CLIMBING TEAM, please notice that the Dartmouth Climbing Gym and Dartmouth Climbing Team are two separate entities. Management does not directly oversee the Climbing Team, and vice versa. If you would like to contact them, please reach out to them at Dartmouth.Climbing.Team@dartmouth.edu. Thanks!*
